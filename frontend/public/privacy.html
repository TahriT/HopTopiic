Privacy Policy

HopTopicc is designed with privacy as a default. This policy explains what data is collected, where it is stored, and how it is used.

1. Summary

HopTopicc collects no personal data on its servers. The app runs entirely in your browser. Speech is processed locally by the browser's built-in Web Speech API and never leaves your device via HopTopicc.

2. Data Stored in Your Browser

The following items are stored in your browser's localStorage only:

KeyContentsWhen cleared
hoptopicc-local-modeWhether Local Mode is active (true/false)On browser clear
hoptopicc-local-mic-idYour selected microphone device IDOn browser clear
hoptopicc-server-urlBackend server address (Backend Mode only)On browser clear
hoptopicc-discord-webhookDiscord webhook URL (if you connected a channel)On disconnect or browser clear
hoptopicc-discord-channelDiscord channel name (display only)On disconnect or browser clear

All of the above stays on your device and is never transmitted to HopTopicc.

3. Microphone & Speech

In Local Mode, your microphone audio is processed entirely by the browser's Web Speech API (provided by your browser vendor, e.g. Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge). HopTopicc never receives, stores, or transmits raw audio or transcripts.

Please review your browser vendor's privacy policy to understand how the Web Speech API handles audio on their infrastructure.

4. Discord Integration

If you connect a Discord channel, HopTopicc will POST topic-change notifications to the webhook URL you authorised via Discord's OAuth2 flow. These messages contain:

  • The inferred topic label (derived from keyword frequency — not verbatim speech)
  • A timestamp
  • The trigger type (keyword drift or transition phrase)

No audio, full transcripts, or personally identifying information are included in Discord posts. The webhook URL is stored only in your browser's local storage.

5. Third-Party Services

  • GitHub Pages — hosts the static app files. GitHub may log IP addresses and HTTP headers per their privacy policy.
  • Discord — only if you use the Discord integration. Messages are governed by Discord's Privacy Policy.

6. Analytics & Tracking

HopTopicc does not use cookies, analytics scripts, tracking pixels, or any third-party analytics tools.

7. Children's Privacy

HopTopicc is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

9. Contact

Questions or concerns? Open an issue at github.com/TahriT/HopTopicc .
